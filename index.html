FFXIV Tribe tracker

This calculator allows you to track how many quests and days you need to max the Allied society ranks.

For this we assume 3 quests per tribe daily.

The total days is a sum of the highest amount of days for every 4 tribes (12 allowances per day, 3 per tribe)
